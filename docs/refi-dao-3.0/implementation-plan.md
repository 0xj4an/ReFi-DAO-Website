## 7. Risk Management

### 7.1 Potential Risks

**Documentation Risks:**
- Documents may become outdated quickly
- Information may be incomplete
- Sources may be missing

**Mitigation:**
- Regular update schedule
- Source citations required
- Review process established

**Network Validation Risks:**
- Low participation in validation
- Conflicting feedback
- Delayed decision-making

**Mitigation:**
- Multiple feedback channels
- Clear decision framework
- Timeline with deadlines

**Implementation Risks:**
- Timeline delays
- Resource constraints
- Changing priorities

**Mitigation:**
- Flexible timeline
- Regular reassessment
- Priority-based approach

### 7.2 Contingency Plans

**If Validation Delayed:**
- Extend feedback period
- Provide additional channels
- Escalate to Core Stewards

**If Models Need Major Revision:**
- Allow for model refinement period
- Update comparison matrix
- Re-present to network

**If Implementation Delayed:**
- Adjust timeline
- Prioritize critical tasks
- Communicate changes

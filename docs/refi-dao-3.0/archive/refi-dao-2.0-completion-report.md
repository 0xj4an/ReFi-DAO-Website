# ReFi DAO 2.0 Completion Report

**Completion Date:** October 2025  
**Status:** Complete  
**Purpose:** Final report documenting the completion of ReFi DAO 2.0 infrastructure-building phase

---

## Executive Summary

ReFi DAO 2.0 successfully completed its infrastructure-building phase (2024-H2 2025), establishing a fully operational network society with on-chain roles, conviction voting, clear governance, and proven pathways for participation.

**Mission Accomplished:** Evolved from movement to operational infrastructure

**Transition:** Now transitioning to operational scaling, launching Network Initiatives, activating revenue streams, and preparing for integration with Regenerative.fi and $ReFi token launch in Q1 2026.

## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well

- **Biannual Renewal Process:** High retention rate (92%) demonstrates strong community engagement
- **Gardens Funding Pools:** Successfully funded new nodes, demonstrating effective funding mechanism
- **Documentation Hub:** Comprehensive resources enable clear participation pathways
- **On-Chain Infrastructure:** Hats Protocol and Guild.xyz integration provides operational foundation

### Challenges Identified

- **Financial Sustainability:** 3-5 month runway requires revenue activation
- **Grant Pipeline:** Weak H2 grant pipeline creates risk
- **Operational Capacity:** Limited capacity for simultaneous initiatives
- **Node Support:** Need for systematic node support identified

### Opportunities

- **Service Fees:** DD24/localism.fund round management
- **Partnerships:** Regenerative.fi and Prosperity Pass SOWs
- **Sponsorships:** Research & Media Initiative outputs
- **Network Growth:** Strong foundation for scaling

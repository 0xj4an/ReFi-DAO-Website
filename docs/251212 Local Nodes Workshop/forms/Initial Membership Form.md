# Local Node Formation Interest Form

**Platform:** Tally  
**Purpose:** Initial application to start a ReFi Local Node

## Form Structure

### Introduction Text

> **Welcome to ReFi DAO's Local Node Network!**
> 
> Thank you for your interest in starting a Local Node in your region. This form collects the essential information we need to support your journey.
> 
> By submitting this form, you're taking the first step toward becoming an officially recognized ReFi Local Node. After submission:
> 1. You'll receive a confirmation email with next steps
> 2. Join ReFi DAO's Garden (if you haven't already)
> 3. Submit your proposal on the Regen Coordination Hub forum
> 
> **Time to complete:** ~5 minutes

## Section 2: Local Node Information

### 2.1 Local Node Name / Region

### 2.2 Country
- **Field type:** Dropdown (country list)
- **Required:** Yes

### 2.3 City/Region
- **Field type:** Short text
- **Required:** Yes

### 2.4 Brief Description of Your Local Node Vision
- **Field type:** Long text
- **Required:** Yes
- **Placeholder:** "In 2-3 sentences, describe what you envision for your Local Node and what makes your region unique for regenerative finance..."
- **Character limit:** 500

### 2.5 Your Background/Experience
- **Field type:** Long text
- **Required:** No

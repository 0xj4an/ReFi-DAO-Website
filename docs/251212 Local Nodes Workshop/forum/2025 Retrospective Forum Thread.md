### Key Insights

1. **Global South advantage** ‚Äî Crypto has clear use cases where currencies are unstable. Harder to convince people in Europe where finance "works."

2. **Open source saves money** ‚Äî Website template costs $10/year vs. hundreds monthly on SaaS.

3. **Physical presence builds trust** ‚Äî Living in the community long-term was key to getting cooperatives on board.

4. **Team diversity matters** ‚Äî Successful nodes have complementary skills (technical, networking, coordination).

5. **"ReFi" is happening without us** ‚Äî People doing regenerative work on the ground who've never heard of Web3. Our job is to connect and support.

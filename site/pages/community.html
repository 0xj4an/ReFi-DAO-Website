Collaboration

Regen Coordination

A collaborative effort to coordinate across the regenerative ecosystem.

Regen Coordination is an initiative focused on improving coordination between regenerative projects, DAOs, and communities. By facilitating communication and resource sharing, we help the ecosystem work together more effectively.

The initiative brings together leaders from across the regenerative space to share learnings, align on strategy, and collaborate on shared challenges. Through regular calls, working groups, and collaborative projects, we're building the connective tissue that enables collective action.

Key Focus Areas

  • Cross-ecosystem coordination and communication
  • Shared governance frameworks and standards
  • Collaborative funding and resource allocation
  • Ecosystem mapping and knowledge sharing
